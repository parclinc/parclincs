Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how ParkingInLincoln collects, uses, and protects any information that you provide when using this website.

ParkingInLincoln is committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected.

Information We Collect

We do not require users to create an account or provide personal information in order to use the website.

However, some information may be collected automatically through third-party services such as analytics or advertising tools.

Analytics

This website uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site. This may collect information such as:

  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on the site
  • General location (city or country level)
  • Device and browser type

This information is used only to improve the website and user experience.

Advertising

ParkingInLincoln uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google and its partners may use cookies to show ads based on your visits to this and other websites.

You can learn more about how Google uses data from sites that use its services by visiting Google’s privacy policies.

Affiliate Links

Some links on this website may be affiliate links. This means that ParkingInLincoln may earn a small commission if you click a link and make a booking or purchase.

This may include services such as parking space providers or hotel booking platforms. These external websites have their own privacy policies and we do not accept responsibility for their content or policies.

Cookies

Cookies may be used by third-party services such as Google Analytics or Google AdSense to improve functionality and advertising relevance.

You can choose to accept or decline cookies through your browser settings.

External Links

This website may contain links to other websites, such as parking providers or hotel booking platforms. Once you leave this site, we do not have control over those websites and cannot be responsible for their privacy practices.
