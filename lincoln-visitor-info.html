Lincoln Visitor Information

Essential tips for visiting Lincoln including arrival times, parking advice, and how long to spend exploring the city.

Best Time to Arrive

Lincoln city centre can get busy, especially on weekends and during tourist season.

  • Best arrival time: before 10:00 AM
  • Busiest period: 11:30 AM – 3:00 PM
  • Quieter time: before 10:30AM, after 4:00 PM
Tip: Arrive before 10:00AM on weekends to avoid crowds and secure better parking.

How Long to Spend in Lincoln

Most visitors spend around 3–5 hours exploring the historic centre.

Typical visit schedule:
  • 1–2 hours exploring Lincoln Cathedral
  • 1–2 hours visiting Lincoln Castle
  • 1 hour walking down Steep Hill and the High Street
  • Extra time for cafés, shops, and museums

Parking Tips

Parking near Lincoln's historic area can be limited, especially close to the cathedral.

  • St Paul's Lane Short Stay Car Park (3 hours max) – good location for castle and cathedral
  • Motherby Lane – slightly cheaper option near top of High Street
  • Broadgate – cheapest parking but located further from High Street

Walking Around Lincoln

The historic area around the cathedral and castle is located uphill from the main shopping district.

  • Steep Hill connects the High Street to the cathedral quarter
  • The walk takes around 10–15 minutes
  • The route is steep but full of historic buildings and shops
