Free Parking in Lincoln

All on-street parking in Lincoln city centre is free on Sundays.

Outside meter hours (usually after 6 pm, Mon–Sat), parking in many bays and council car parks is also free overnight.

Check signage carefully — some restrictions may still apply, especially in permit zones or special event areas.

Tips for Free Parking

  • Always check street signs before leaving your car
  • Some bays are resident-only even on Sundays — avoid fines
  • Arrive early near popular spots like the Cathedral or Castle to secure a space
  • Walking from free on-street bays is often faster than paid car parks on busy days
